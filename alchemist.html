Alchemist Accelerator

Alchemist Accelerator is one of the top accelerator programs in Silicon Valley exclusively working with enterprise-focused start ups.

Brief

Alchemist was facing new competition and trying to find ways to stay lean and maintain quality of the program while growing. The challenge was to build tools that improve the internal operations of the accelerator and make the business more scalable.

Hypotheses

  • Introductions are a core function of the accelerator that is time consuming, costly and not scalable.
  • Founders are relentless entrepreneurs and will shamelessly reach out to anyone that they think might help them.
  • People in the Alchemist network are high worth and very busy making them sensitive to being spammed.

Findings

The majority of introductions are requests from founders to get introduced to someone in the Alchemist network of 15,000+ mentors, investors and corporate customers.

Alchemist creates a lot of value for founders by proactively sharing their portfolio companies with investors and corporate partners.

Core Themes

  • Reliable, up-to-date data is crucial to help founders target the right people.
  • The Alchemist brand is a valuable asset towards growing the network and sourcing this data.
  • Capturing feedback on calls and meetings between founders and the network is valuable for other founders and the Alchemist internal team.

Results

Vault for Founders

An Automated Introduction System with the ability to search by interests, expertise an more from the 15,000+ investors, mentors and corporate partners in the Alchemist network. Alchemist Network Members complete a registration form to join and receive requests from founders, which they can accept or deny after viewing the founder's message and information.

Alchemist Vault for Internal Team

An Automated Introduction System wherer the Alchemist internal team can send list of companies to investors and corporate partners. They are then able to review and select which companies they would like to get introduced to or meet with.
